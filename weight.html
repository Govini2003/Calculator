Do You Know?

Grams (g): The basic unit of mass in the metric system.

Kilograms (kg): Equals 1,000 grams. Commonly used for everyday measurements.

Tonnes: Equals 1,000,000 grams or 1,000 kilograms. Used for measuring large masses.

Pounds (lbs): Equals 453.592 grams. Commonly used in the United States.
