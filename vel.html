Do You Know?

Meters per second (m/s): The SI unit for velocity, often used in physics to express speed.


Kilometers per hour (km/h): Commonly used in everyday life, especially in transportation. 1 m/s is equal to 3.6 km/h.


Miles per hour (mph): A unit of speed used primarily in the United States and the United Kingdom. 1 mph is approximately 1.609 km/h.


Speed of Light: The ultimate speed limit of the universe, approximately 299,792,458 meters per second or about 186,282 miles per hour.
