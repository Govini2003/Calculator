Temperature Converter


Temperature conversion is the process of changing the value of temperature from one unit to another.
Now you can convert any temperature easily with using Cool Cal.



Temperature Conversion Formulas

To convert Fahrenheit to Celsius, we use the formula: F = 1.8C + 32.

To convert Celsius to Fahrenheit, we use the formula: C = (F - 32) / 1.8.

To convert Kelvin to Celsius, we use the formula: C = K - 273.15.

To convert Celsius to Kelvin, we use the formula: K = C + 273.15.





Do You Know?

The average human body temperature is around 37°C.

Here are some interesting facts:

  • Body temperature can vary depending on the time of day, the weather, and your activity level.
  • Normal body temperature can range from about 36.1°C to 37.2°C (97°F to 99°F).
  • A fever is generally considered to be a body temperature above 38°C (100.4°F).
  • Temperature regulation is crucial for maintaining good health and is controlled by the hypothalamus in the brain.

Do You Know?

Absolute zero is the lowest temperature possible. At a temperature of absolute zero there is no motion and no heat. Absolute zero occurs at a temperature of 0 kelvin, or -273.15 degrees Celsius, or at -460 degrees Fahrenheit.
