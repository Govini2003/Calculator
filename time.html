Do You Know?

Seconds (s): The base unit of time in the International System of Units (SI). There are 86,400 seconds in a day.

Minutes (min): Equals 60 seconds. Commonly used to measure short durations of time.

Hours (h): Equals 60 minutes or 3,600 seconds. There are 24 hours in a day.

Days: Equals 24 hours or 1,440 minutes. It's the most common unit for measuring the duration of events or activities.
