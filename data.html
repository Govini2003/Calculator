Do You Know?

For memory and file sizes, a megabyte is 1,048,576 bytes, not 1,000,000 bytes! Similarly, a kilobyte is 1,024 bytes, not 1,000, and a gigabyte is 1,073,741,824 bytes, not 1,000,000,000. These numbers are all 'powers of 2', which means 2 multiplied by itself a number of times.



Do You Know?

A yottabyte is the largest unit approved as a standard size by the International System of Units (SI). The yottabyte is about 1 septillion bytes -- or, as an integer, 1,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000 bytes.
